📻 Radio Ninja Pirata 💀

Streaming dynamic playlists on Spotify since 2020!

Radio Ninja Pirata is born from our passion for listening to random music. Our project is unique! We created a new style of playlists. We call them dynamic playlists 👇 👇 👇

Â¡Our dynamic playlists change every 2 hours with 40 new random tracks!

We are tired of playlists featuring only the "best songs" from the "top 10 best artists". In Radio Ninja Pirata, we grant no privileges. All artists we feature have the same chance to be selected, and we consider their whole discography - not only the "hits".

Radio Ninja Pirata is a genuinely egalitarian radio. We want you to discover new bands and new songs. We want less-known bands to reach a broader audience.

We fine-tune our playlists for a seamless listening experience with the minimum jumps in style. We have many playlists for the different genres we love and are creating more.

Our playlists work as regular Spotify playlists: you can save them or make songs available offline. We made the playlists small (40 tracks) to be easy to handle. The magic? All songs will change every two hours. Some playlists have dozens, others hundreds, and others thousands of artists.

Radio Ninja Pirata is free and without advertisements. As you can see, no 💩 add banners around this page. 😈

Radio Ninja Pirata is made by humans and for humans. All our playlists are curated by us, the people! 🫂
