# Stoner Rock

Go high and to the depths with our **Stoner Rock** radio station!

Our stations are dynamic playlists on Spotify. How are they dynamic? Because they regenerate every 2h with 40 new songs! Songs are selected randomly from the catalogue of artists :-) Discover more about [our project in our home page](https://radioninjapirata.github.io).

Did we miss someone? Is your band not featured in our radio? [Let us know!](https://github.com/RadioNinjaPirata/commentsENG/issues/new)
